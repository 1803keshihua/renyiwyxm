The UCAA was founded in 2013 with the purpose of bridging the gap between Chinese and British creative and advertising industries to allow greater collaboration and benefit both industries through cultural, creative, technical and financial exchange. It is an international and bilingual association headquartered in London and Beijing.

The UCAA host events throughout the year in order to: maintain close contact with its members;encourage co-operation between its members;train members to further develop skills relating to media and advertising industries

The UCAA is working to promote the UK as a logical place to start international expansion, highlighting the country's cultural diversity and business-friendly environment.

UCAA welcomes a variety of members, including but not limited to Advertising Agencies, Production Companies, Modelling Agencies, Photographers, Celebrities, Local and International Brands, Studios, Film Companies, Equipment Rental Companies, Independent Producers, Academics and more.
